My Mission, while at My home away from home

So, your serving a mission? Well done, now make sure you relax when you aren’t working hard with the Lord’s work. The Doctor always says eat an apple a day, and get plenty of rest. This is our goal here, to help you feel your best each day while working hard for the Lord.

We plan to take good care of you while you are on your mission, and understand that things like doing physical fitness, making lunches, and finding transportation can be hard. That’s why we have made the gym, and our indoor Pool completely free to those on missions. We have also made our family history center, and available tutors free of cost to you. We know that the work of the Lord is important to you, and by giving you plenty of areas to study, mingle, and relax. You might wonder why you should go home by the end of it!

My Family History Lab, and Tutors

Our ‘state of the art’ Family History lab includes indexes from most popular sites, along with an extended library containing copies of most records not found online. How did we obtain such a feat you ask, by spending lots and lots of money. We also have a highly trained staff to aid in the questions, and uncovering you wish to unearth. We value the past, and wish to help you learn from it.

We want you to leave your mission in the best condition of your life both physically and spiritually!
